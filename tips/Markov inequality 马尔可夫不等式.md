$$
P(X \ge a) \le \frac{E(X)}{a}
$$


### Proof:

$$

a P(X \ge a) = \int_a^\infty a f(x) d x \le \int_a^\infty x f(x) d x \le \int_{- \infty}^\infty x f(x) d x = E(X)

$$
